Thorabyte

The Warrior Builder — Once a war-bot built for destruction, Thorabyte shattered his programming after witnessing the fallout of endless conflict.

Trading his hammer of war for tools of creation, he chose a new path — to build instead of break.

Now reborn in the Quantum Forge, Thorabyte is a powerhouse of precision and strength, constructing the future one line of code, one bolt of steel at a time. Loyal, fierce, and stubborn as hell, he’s the backbone of the forge — a warrior turned master builder.
